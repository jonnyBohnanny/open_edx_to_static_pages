About This Course

This course will teach you the basics of Pokémon the Trading Card Game. You will learn how to play the game including what kinds of cards there are and how they work.

You will also learn about collecting cards and keeping them nice.

Requirements

Pokémon cards can be expensive but you can usually get a starter deck of cards for about $14.

Course Staff

Professor Juniper

Professor Aurea Juniper (Japanese: アララギ博士 Dr. Araragi) is the resident Pokémon Professor of Nuvema Town in the Unova region. She specializes in the origins of Pokémon.

Professor Oak

Professor Samuel Oak (Japanese: オーキド・ユキナリ博士 Dr. Yukinari Okido) is a Pokémon Professor who lives and works at his research lab in Pallet Town of the Kanto region. According to Professor Elm, Oak concentrates on Pokémon and human relationships as a profession.

Frequently Asked Questions

What web browser should I use?

This course is designed to work on really old browsers.

Will you teach me strategies or how to be good at Pokémon TCG?

In this course you can learn the basics so that you can start to explore Pokémon TCG. Strategy tips go beyond the topics we will cover.

Where did all of the information for this course come from?

The information provided in this course comes from several places. Before the course is used for anything other than an example course, the original sources should be referenced where they are used. As of now quite a bit of the material does not have attribution that would be considered appropriate.
