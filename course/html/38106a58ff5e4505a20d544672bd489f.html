New features constantly appear in the Pokémon TCG. With the release of Pokémon Gold and Silver video games, the Neo Series (the second series of Pokémon Trading Card Game expansions, started bringing several new Pokémon into play. The Metal and Darkness types of Pokémon cards were also introduced, along with Light Pokémon and Dark Pokémon. These were eventually discontinued to prevent confusion with the Darkness type, but returned briefly with the release of the Neo Destiny expansion.
