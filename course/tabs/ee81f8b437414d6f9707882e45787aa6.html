Pokémon Cards

There are several different categories of Pokémon cards. Each  has a different use in the game. A good deck will usually have a mix consisting of Trainers, Energy and Pokemon. These will typically be mixed in about equal proportions in a deck. This means a typical deck will have 20 Pokémon cards, 20 Trainer cards and, 20 Energy Cards. Set Number and Set Symbol

The Set Symbol on a Pokémon card indicates what set the card is from. The set symbol is located on the bottom of the card near the card number and rarity logo.

Card Number, sometimes called Collector Number, is located at the bottom right of the card. This number tells you the position of that car d in a particular set. The number is represented as the card number followed by a slash and then the total number of cards in the set. If the first number, the card number, is higher than the number of total cards in the set the card is considered a secret rare card.
